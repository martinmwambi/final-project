This system provides information about common diseases affecting livestock specifically cattle, goats and sheep; the symptoms of the diseases and how to control/treat them.

The system also enables veterinary doctors to register themselves and provide their contact details in order to be able to be reached to by farmers.

The system also enables farmers to access the contact details of veterinary doctors who live nearby their homes in order to get quick help for their livestock when seriously ill.

The system also gives farmers information about important meals to give their livestock to improve their health.

Information about livestock diseases and important livestock meals can be accessed by using the search bar at the Help section.
